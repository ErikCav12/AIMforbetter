Our Syllabus

Modular training, tailored to your organisation

Organisations are at different stages in their AI journey. AIM doesn't deliver a one-size-fits-all course. Instead, we provide a modular approach that allows you to build a programme aligned to your specific priorities.

Each course is designed by selecting from our range of Learning AIMs and Objectives, ensuring training develops the capabilities, behaviours, and management approaches your organisation needs.

How It Works

Select objectives. We build the programme.

6

Selecting fewer objectives enables deeper exploration of each topic. Selecting more allows broader coverage. This approach calibrates each programme to your desired balance between depth and breadth.
