Our flagship programme

As organisations prepare for the next generation of early-career professionals, many are recognising the importance of establishing strong foundations for responsible AI use from the outset.

Flagship Full-Day Course

Responsible AI Use for Early-Career Professionals

A full-day course that gives early-career employees the judgement and practical skills to use AI tools well, spot what they get wrong, and be open about how AI supports their work.

Full day In-person or online 6 learning objectives Interactive

Learning Objectives Covered

This course covers six objectives across Capability and Behaviour:

Understand how generative AI works, where it adds value, and where it introduces risk. Recognise the professional consequences of relying on AI outputs without appropriate scrutiny.

Develop practical techniques to identify errors, challenge assumptions, and validate AI-generated content, supporting ownership of output quality.

Build awareness of governance, confidentiality, and data-security considerations when using AI tools, including appropriate handling of sensitive or proprietary information.

Understand the differences between publicly available and organisation-specific AI tools, enabling informed decisions about when and how AI should be used.

Develop practical skills in structuring prompts, refining outputs, and interacting productively with AI tools to support high-quality work.

Encourage open and responsible communication around AI use, supporting trust, consistency, and shared understanding across teams.
